Property Overview

A Well-Positioned Center at Savannah's Midtown Crossroads

DeRenne Shopping Center occupies the northwest corner of DeRenne Avenue and Abercorn Street — one of Savannah's most heavily trafficked intersections, carrying approximately ±43,000 vehicles per day. The center's position at this signalized corner provides exceptional visibility and access from both major arterials.

The 46,300 SF neighborhood retail center features a diverse, established tenant mix including national healthcare, food & beverage, and service retail. The center benefits from abundant surface parking, multiple ingress and egress points, and a prominent multi-tenant pylon sign with excellent street visibility.

DeRenne Avenue serves as Savannah's primary east-west commercial corridor, connecting the Abercorn retail spine to the islands and carrying a consistent mix of commuters, residents, and visitors. The corridor is flanked by national quick-service restaurants, fuel stations, and neighborhood-serving retail — creating a high-frequency consumer environment that benefits all tenants.

With three suites available from ±800 to ±3,500 SF, the center accommodates a wide range of retail, food & beverage, health & wellness, professional, and service concepts. All spaces are available for immediate occupancy under negotiable lease terms.

Lease Structure

Transparent, Market-Standard Lease Terms

All available suites are offered on a Base Rent plus NNN (triple net) structure. The tenant pays a negotiated base rent plus their pro-rata share of the center's operating expenses — a standard, predictable structure for retail tenants.

What the Tenant Pays

Base Rent — Negotiated rate per square foot per year, paid monthly
Common Area Maintenance (CAM) — Pro-rata share of parking lot, landscaping, lighting, and shared area upkeep
Property Taxes — Pro-rata share of the center's annual real estate tax assessment
Property Insurance — Pro-rata share of the landlord's building and liability insurance premium
Suite Utilities — Tenant's own electric, gas, and water usage within the leased premises

Lease Terms at a Glance

Lease Rate: Negotiable — contact broker for current market guidance
Lease Type: Base Rent + Pro-Rata NNN (CAM, Taxes, Insurance)
Renewal Options: Available — terms negotiable
Sublease: Not permitted without landlord consent
Availability: Immediate — all three suites ready for occupancy

The NNN structure means the landlord's base rent is net of operating expenses — giving tenants full transparency into their total occupancy cost. Pro-rata shares are calculated based on each tenant's leased square footage as a percentage of the total leasable area. Contact the listing broker for current NNN estimates and a detailed cost breakdown.
